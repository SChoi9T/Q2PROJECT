Longganisa Silog


Image Source!
Ingredients
Measurements Ingredients
3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 tsp salt
2 pcs large eggs (1 per serving)
4-6 pcs longganisa meat
Instructions
  1. Prick the longganisa with a fork to release oil while cooking. Place them in a pan with a little water and cook over medium heat until the water evaporates. Allow them to fry in their own fat until golden brown.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Add minced garlic and saut√© until golden and aromatic. Add the day-old rice and stir-fry until well combined with the garlic. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the eggs sunny-side up or to your preference.
  4. On a plate, serve a portion of garlic fried rice, one fried egg, and your chosen protein (tapa, tocino, or longganisa).
  5. Serve hot with sliced tomatoes, atchara (pickled papaya), or vinegar dipping sauce on the side. Enjoy your meal!
