Halo-Halo


Image Source!
Ingredients
Measurements Ingredients
1 cup shaved ice
1/2 cup evaporated milk
2 tbsp sweetened red mung beans
2 tbsp sweetened kidney beans
2 tbsp sweetened jackfruit, sliced thinly
2 tbsp macapuno (sweetened coconut strips)
2 tbsp sweetened banana
2 tbsp nata de coco (coconut jelly)
2 tbsp kaong (sugar palm fruit)
2 tbsp sweetened purple yam
1 scoop ube ice cream
10-15 grams toasted pinipig (pounded rice flakes) or crispy rice cereal
Instructions
  1. In a tall glass or dessert bowl, layer the sweetened fillings (mung beans, kidney beans, jackfruit, macapuno, bananas, nata de coco, kaong, and ube halaya).
  2. Fill the glass or bowl with finely shaved ice on top of the sweetened layers.
  3. Drizzle evaporated milk over the shaved ice to moisten it. You can add more or less depending on your preference.
  4. Add a scoop of ube ice cream on top of the shaved ice. Place a slice of leche flan beside the ice cream. Sprinkle toasted pinipig or crispy rice cereal for a crunchy texture.
  5. Serve immediately with a long spoon. Mix all the ingredients before eating to enjoy the full halo-halo experience!
