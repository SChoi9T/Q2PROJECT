Champorado


Image Source!
Ingredients
Measurements Ingredients
1 cup glutinous rice
4 cups water
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
1 pinch salt
1 bowl evaporated or condensed milk
Instructions
  1. Wash the glutinous rice thoroughly and drain.
  2. In a pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the rice and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Dissolve the cocoa powder in a small amount of warm water, then add it to the pot. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add sugar and a pinch of salt. Stir and adjust sweetness as desired.
  5. Cook until the rice is soft and the mixture has thickened to your liking.
  6. Serve warm with a drizzle of evaporated or condensed milk on top. Enjoy your meal!
