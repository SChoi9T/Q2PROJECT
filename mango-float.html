Mango Float (Crema de Mangga)


Image Source!
Ingredients
Measurements Ingredients
3 pcs large ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced into thin strips
1 pack graham crackers (about 200-250g)
2 cups heavy cream or all-purpose cream
1 can condensed milk
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
Instructions
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, condensed milk, and evaporated milk. Whip the mixture using a hand mixer or stand mixer until it thickens and soft peaks form (about 5-7 minutes). Optionally, add vanilla extract to enhance the flavor.
  2. In a rectangular or square dish (usually a 9x9-inch or 9x13-inch dish works well), layer the graham crackers to cover the bottom of the dish. You may need to break some pieces of graham crackers to fit.
  3. Spread a layer of the whipped cream mixture over the graham crackers, smoothing it out with a spatula. Arrange a layer of mango slices on top of the cream. Repeat the layers (graham crackers, cream, and mango) until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of the whipped cream mixture on top.
  4. Cover the dish with plastic wrap or foil and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight. This will allow the flavors to meld together and the graham crackers to soften and absorb the cream.
  5. Before serving, garnish the top with extra mango slices for decoration. Slice into squares and enjoy your meal!
