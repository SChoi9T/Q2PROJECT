Adobo


Image Source!
Ingredients
Measurements Ingredients
1 kg chicken or pork (or a mix), cut into serving pieces
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup vinegar
1 cup water
1 head garlic, minced
1 pc onion, chopped (optional)
2-3 pcs bay leaves
1 tsp peppercorns (or ground black pepper)
1 tbsp cooking oil
1-2 tsp sugar (optional)
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken or pork in soy sauce, garlic, and pepper for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onion (if using) until translucent. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in the marinade, vinegar, and water. Add bay leaves and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat and simmer, uncovered, until the meat is tender and the sauce has reduced.
  5. Add sugar if you prefer a slightly sweet flavor. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your meal!
